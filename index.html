Got Questions

Frequently Asked

Who is this training for? ▼
Anyone preparing for Maharashtra Police, Army, or any government job that requires a physical fitness test. Both male and female aspirants are welcome. No prior experience needed.
How do I pay for the camp? ▼
Pay via UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) to our UPI ID shown in the Payment section. After payment, send screenshot on WhatsApp and your seat is confirmed within minutes.
What should I bring to the camp? ▼
Sports shoes, comfortable running clothes, water bottle, and a towel. We handle everything else — training plan, evaluation, and feedback. Come ready to work hard.
Is refund available if I can't attend? ▼
Yes — if you inform us at least 48 hours before the camp, we'll transfer your registration to the next available camp date. Full refund available within 7 days if requested before camp starts.
How is the online plan different from camp? ▼
The online plan is for aspirants who can't travel to a camp. You get a 4-week daily training schedule, video guides, weekly check-in calls, and access to our private Telegram group for support.
Will there be camps in my district? ▼
We regularly host camps across Maharashtra — Pune, Nashik, Aurangabad, and more. WhatsApp us your district and we'll notify you when a camp is scheduled near you.
